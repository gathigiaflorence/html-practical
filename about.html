Our Story

History of Sports Physiotherapy in Kenya

Sports physiotherapy started gaining recognition in Kenya in the late 1990s, driven by a need to improve athlete recovery and reduce sports-related injuries. Over the years, technology has played a huge role in bridging the communication gap between coaches, physios, and athletes.

Our Goals

  • Enhance athlete injury recovery times.
  • Improve collaboration between coaches and physiotherapists.
  • Collect accurate data for better sports performance analysis.

Steps We Follow for Athlete Recovery

  1. Initial assessment and injury logging.
  2. Physiotherapy treatment and progress tracking.
  3. Follow-up evaluation and performance monitoring.

Key Terms

Rehabilitation
The process of restoring an athlete to peak performance after an injury.
Preventive Therapy
Exercises and programs aimed at reducing the risk of sports injuries.
Follow-up Session
A scheduled meeting between the athlete and physiotherapist to track healing progress.
